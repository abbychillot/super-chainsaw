THE LIFE OF TOM.

Tom has had quite the life filled with many accomplishments, changes, and a lot of risk taking.
Scroll to learn more about his life and all that he has done.

  • Youth Years 1971-1984
    Tom was born in 1971 in a small town in the suburbs of Pennsylvania. His family consists of his mother, father, and three older brothers. Growing up, he attend St. Gabriel's Roman Catholic School in Stowe, PA. Because he had such a strict upbringing in the classroom, he was able to make a lot of friends in his classes that he was able to relate to. While attending a Catholic school, he was able to develop a conscience and eventually learn to do the right thing and fix any wrongs.
    Youth Years Photo Gallery
  • Teen Years 1985-1989
    After attending Catholic primary school, Tom attending Catholic high school as well. One hobby Tom took on was sports, which would eventually become a big part of his life. His high school was small, so playing aginst much bigger schools allowed for a big challenge. This is where he would start to learn a lot of valuable life lessons he would carry throughout the rest of his life. He was told by both his father and coaches that "effort offsets talent," which he still applies to his life almost 30 years later.
    Teen Years Photo Gallery
  • Young Adult Years 1989-1995
    After high school, Tom attended Dickinson College to play Division 3 football. He did both his undergrad and grad at Dickinson, and earned his MBA by the time he was 24. As a student, Tom also was named president of the Raven's Claw Senior Men Honorary Society. Playing college football was a big part of Tom's life, so big that he ended up being named as captain for the team his senior year. After undergrad, Tom was named one of the coaches of Dickinson's football team. He coached the team while still manageing to earn his MBA. This is where he started to realize what he loved doing in life: being a role model to others. He is said to use the skills he's acquired over time in his professional life and his parenting life.

  • Adult Years 1995-2001
    After graduating from college, Tom began working for Boise Cascade. He found himeself being successful, yet was not what he wanted to do for a career. He felt humiliated at work and wanted to get out of there as soon as possible. He learned with this job he did not want to work for big companies. He did learn some valuable life lessons such as how to take risks,sacrifices, and betting on himself to be central to accomplishment.
    Adult Years Photo Gallery
  • Mature Adult Years 2001-2022
    Back in 2001, Tom and his family moved from Pennsylvania to Northern Virginia, where he currently still lives as of today. He found himself taking more risks by moving to a new city, becoming a new parent, and getting a new job. Living and working outside of Washington D.C. was super beneficial, as it is known to be a "melting pot," which he didn't experience in Pennsylvania. He began working for TMA Direct, where he took the risk of tying his compensation to the company earnings. Because of this, he was able to save for both his children's college education plus all other major expenses along the way. He has continued to remain debt free after deciding to take a chance on himself. In 2023, Tom will making one of the biggest moves of his life by starting up his very own company.
